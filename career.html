  • Position - UI/UX Designer

    No of Positions - 1 nos.

    Experience - 6 + Years

    Location - Latin America

    Job Description:

    As a UI/UX Designer, you are responsible for envisioning and creating the core web and desktop application experiences. Your role requires continuous collaboration with users, stakeholders, developers, and product owners to create user focused and tested solutions for our business needs. As an integral part of the team, you will help shape the UI/UX identity across the company and drive the implementation of design values into our core processes.

    About the role:

    • Clearly articulate UI/UX processes
    • Create sitemaps, wireframes, user flows, and process flows
    • Participate in user research, testing and provide solutions based on findings
    • Create design systems for distinct users across platforms
    • Engage and brainstorm with business analysts, developers, and stakeholders during design and interaction discussions
    • Continuously integrate user feedback and testing during design lifeline
    • Conduct user testing, provide clear results from test, and suggestions for what to improve
    • Comfortable presenting to colleagues and departments heads about design choices supported by user research
    • Develop visually rich mock-ups and UI assets in accordance with company style guide standards

    Minimum Qualifications:

    • 6+ years as a UI/UX designer
    • Bachelor’s in graphic design or similar work experience
    • Experience with Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, Microsoft Visio (or any other flow-chart software)
    • Able to work in an agile environment that is constantly evolving
    • Ability to take constructive criticism, quickly recover, and pivot based on feedback
    • Team player who can work in collaborative environment
    • Strong communication and organizational skills
