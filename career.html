  • Position - Sr. Automation QA Engineer

    Experience - 4-6 Years

    Location - Pune

    Job Description:

    • experience with industry standard QA tools for test case development, test execution, defect management and web debugging.
    • The engineer is expected to work with a team and to work independently with equal proficiency.
    • The associate will interact with his or her supervisor as needed, but many activities will require independent thought and decision making.
    • Analyze and understand requirements, devise a test strategy to adequately cover the requirements
    • develop high-level test scenarios & test scripts that implement the strategy, that meet coding standards and quality
    • Own the QA role, this includes but is not limited to: consume requirements, write and execute tests (manual and automated), monitor testing progress, report status, log defects, track and test defect fixes, choose when and what to re-test, collaborate with team members, monitor project progress, incorporate schedule and scope changes, shepherd the product to delivery, be responsible for quality of product
    • Participate in team process improvement. Understand our development lifecycle, participate in its continuous improvement.
    • Excellent knowledge of automated QA systems such Selenium, TestNG, Maven/Eclipse, Docker, GitHub & Jenkins is required
    • Excellent knowledge of performance test systems needed
    • High level skillset of Java language to create automated test scripts required
    • Experience in audit/code review of code quality a plus
    • Knowledge of industry standard automation repository, process and build required
    • Experience in project mgmt. and defect mgmt. tools in JIRA or other tools required
    • Familiarity with Object Orientated Design and Analysis.
    • Knowledge of and ability to perform in an AGILE environment.
    • Excellent knowledge of software development lifecycle.
    • Excellent knowledge of software testing lifecycle.
    • Able to review system and functional requirements and extract data to use in preparing automated test plans and scripts.
    • Able to meet aggressive deadlines and handle multiple and complex projects.
    • Able to use independent judgment to plan, prioritize and organize a diversified workload.
    • Able to define highly complex automated test cases and scenarios.
    • Coding skills as required by automation tools utilized.
    Roles & Responsibilities
    • Analyzes, designs, develops, and maintains automated scripts and manual regression test plans for a variety of interfaces. Develops automation frameworks consisting of functional libraries while adhering to coding standards.
    • Schedules and conducts testing, submits testing errors and issues to development for correction, tests corrected code and tracks defects.
    • Documents test progress and results. Prepares reports and presents results as needed.
    • Assists in planning, scheduling and implementing automation projects. Monitors automation projects and ensures that projects are completed on schedule and within budget. Performs evaluation of products and systems and determines candidates for automation.
    • Analyzes scripts and performs code reviews of automated scripts. Reports results of automated scripts in test management tools . Provides updates on automation progress and custom reporting as requested.
    • Uses automated Quality Assurance systems to facilitate daily activities.
    • Fulfills load testing requests and sets up appropriate load testing monitors on servers. Executes and analyzes load tests, and reports to product team members.
    • Develops automation strategies that will enhance test execution, manual test coverage and the consistency of regression testing.
    • Provides training and technical assistance to members of the automation team. May assist with training on software enhancements. May cross-train and mentor less experienced QA team members.
    • Keeps abreast of software industry “Best Practices” and trends and communicates new information to QA team.
    • May create and maintain test libraries, databases, and test environments.
    • May create or assist with creation of manual tests.

    • Experience with SQL, SQL tuning and data extraction and modification required.
    • Extensive experience working on Data Management Systems and Extract Transform and load processes.
    • Experience working with popular Software Quality Assurance frameworks and tools.

    • Functional experience with testing strategies including Unit Test, Integration Test, Performance test, regression test, acceptance test etc.
    • Experience working on Unix and windows platforms.
    • Experience working on with SDLC, Agile methodology, Test driven Development, configuration Management and source control.
    • Experience working with Relational database management systems and various programming languages.
    • Experience working with databases and & appliances such as Netezza, HDFS, HIve, Oracle, Sql Server, Talend & Samza.
    • Strong SQL skills for testing ETL data warehouse components
    • Develop tests to address the full stack automation (Map reduce programs, database, web services and UI)
    • Expertise in data warehousing concepts including hands on experience in data mart validation
    • Agile Planning tool (e.g. JIRA) is a plus
    • ETL Tools (e.g. Informatica, Talend or any similar ETL tool)
    • Should be familiar with Java
    • Ability to create test environment frameworks
    • Experience in creating synthetic test data (with tool or otherwise)
    • Ability to find RCA of DB architecture and duplicated datasets in DB frameworks
